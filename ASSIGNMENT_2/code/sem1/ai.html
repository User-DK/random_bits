About the Course

This course aims to acquaint students with the meaning, purpose, scope, applications, and effects of Artificial Intelligence (AI). It covers various AI techniques and their applications in Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Robotics. The course also focuses on understanding and representing knowledge in AI systems and analyzing real-life problems to provide AI-based solutions.

Course Code: 6CS303

Desired Requisites: Data structures, algorithm

Course Objectives

  • To acquaint students with the meaning, purpose, scope, applications, and effects of AI.
  • To solve problems by applying a suitable search method, and AI applications in Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Robotics.
  • To understand and represent knowledge in AI systems.
  • To analyse real life problems and provide solutions by applying AI techniques.

Course Outline

  • Module 1: AI - Inception and Scope
  • Module 2: Problem Solving by Search
  • Module 3: Knowledge Representation & Reasoning-I
  • Module 4: Knowledge Representation & Reasoning-II
  • Module 5: Game playing and Introduction to Planning
  • Module 6: Learning and case study
